Create KPIs

Based on your analysis, create KPIs that provide actionable insights. Here are some examples:

Unsafe Condition Rate:
Formula: (Total number of unsafe conditions / Total number of incidents) x 100
Target: Reduce the rate by 10% within the next quarter

Location-Specific Offense Rate:
Formula: (Total number of offenses at a location / Total number of incidents at that location) x 100
Target: Reduce the rate by 15% at the highest-offense location within the next quarter

Company-Specific Compliance Rate:
Formula: (Total number of compliant companies / Total number of companies) x 100
Target: Increase the rate by 20% within the next quarter

Top 3 Most Common Unsafe Actions:
KPI: Track the top 3 most common unsafe actions and monitor their frequency
Target: Reduce the frequency of the top 2 most common unsafe actions by 20% within the next quarter

Employee Engagement Index:
Formula: (Employee feedback survey score / Total number of employees) x 100
Target: Increase the index by 10% within the next quarter
